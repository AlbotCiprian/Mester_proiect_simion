# SEO and accessibility rules

- Preserve indexable server-rendered content and stable canonical URLs.
- Local pages require real unique value; do not create doorway pages.
- Structured data must be generated from truthful source data.
- Never publish fake ratings, prices, reviews, or service areas.
- Meet WCAG 2.2 AA target: keyboard access, visible focus, contrast, labels, errors, touch targets, and reduced motion.
- Validate heading hierarchy, metadata, hreflang, canonicals, sitemap, robots, and noindex behavior.
